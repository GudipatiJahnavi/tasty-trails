Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken
  • Butter
  • Tomato puree
  • Fresh cream
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Red chilli powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with spices.
  2. Heat butter in a pan.
  3. Add chicken and cook lightly.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook well.
  5. Add cream and garam masala.
  6. Simmer on low flame.
  7. Serve with naan or rice.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Basmati rice
  • Onion
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with spices.
  2. Cook rice separately until 70% done.
  3. Prepare chicken gravy.
  4. Layer rice and chicken in a cooker.
  5. Dum cook on low flame.
  6. Serve with raita.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients.
  2. Add butter and milk.
  3. Prepare smooth batter.
  4. Pour into baking tin.
  5. Bake until cooked.
  6. Cool and serve.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk powder
  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Mix milk powder and flour.
  2. Prepare soft dough.
  3. Make small balls.
  4. Fry on low flame until golden.
  5. Soak in sugar syrup.
  6. Serve warm.

Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Fresh cream
  • Sugar
  • Flavouring (vanilla or chocolate)

Steps

  1. Mix milk, cream, and sugar.
  2. Add flavouring.
  3. Pour into container.
  4. Freeze until set.
  5. Scoop and serve.
